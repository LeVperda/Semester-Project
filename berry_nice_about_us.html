About our company

We are a group of first year Machine Learning and Data Engineering students, studying at the University of Applied Sciences in Rovaniemi. For our spring semester project, LUKE – the Natural Resources Institute Finland has commissioned us to create something using the institute’s data set concerning the growth of different berries in certain areas around the year in Finland. After we had gone through said data, we considered our options about how to use it and turn it into a project and in this case, a website.

Berry picking is widely popular in Finland and other Nordic countries, even so that you can book trips to berry picking spots, which are not free of charge. It’s also rather rare for a country to have resources to offer such a grand and diverse arsenal of berries, mushrooms, flowers, and plants in nature – and because of the Everyman’s Right in Finland also to collect, use and even sell. However, not everyone has access to information about berry picking spots in their area. Either the information is not translated, or the data is too difficult to pick apart and make sense of. We wanted the information for berry picking spots to be available for everyone, so we came up with an idea.

Our vision started as a joke: “Facebook” for berry pickers – A way to promote the self-sufficient way of life of the Finns to newcomers as well. Since even the best data analysts chew through data sets with quite a lot of trouble, our number one priority was to give the user an accessible and practical platform to easily understand the data, being able to use it for their own berry picking trips and on top of that, giving back to the platform with their own data collected, thus growing the database.
